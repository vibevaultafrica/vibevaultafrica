The problem

Every night, thousands of people navigate the space between joy and vulnerability.

Existing safety apps are reactive — they require you to press a button when you are already scared. They don't prevent. They don't hold space.

What is missing is proactive infrastructure — something that makes Cape Town nights feel like Berlin or Tokyo. Safe enough to be intrinsically free. A space that holds you before you need to ask for it.

The solution

Three interlocking layers of care.

VibeVault is the guardian at the intersection of safety, freedom, and nightlife culture. Solar-powered. Always on. Designed for the city at 2am.

Physical safety infrastructure

Smart lockers on high-traffic nightlife and beach routes. Solar-powered, weatherproof, glowing with soft turquoise light. Drop your bag. Charge your phone. Go live your night.

The emergency net

One glowing button: I'm Stranded. A ride home, no questions asked. Wellness essentials always available. Safety for every member, every night — without judgement.

Wellness and connection

Electrolytes, sunblock, phone charging, a retro telephone wellness check-in, and circular seating designed so strangers become a little less strange to each other.

Every detail considered

Built for the real conditions of Cape Town life.

Solar powered

High-efficiency monocrystalline solar with lithium-iron-phosphate battery backup. Runs through Cape Town winters, load shedding, and everything in between.

Wellness vending

No glass. Nothing that can become a weapon. Electrolytes, water, healthy snacks, sunblock and seasonal essentials — only what your body actually needs at 2am.

AI smart screen

Maps, local recommendations, ride ordering and a wellness check-in — all available even when your phone is dead. The city in your hands, always.

Eco-hatch

Drop any plastic bottle and receive instant R2 SnapScan credit. The more Cape Town parties, the more the ocean gets cleaned.

Circular seating

Designed using prospect and refuge theory, the golden ratio, and Third Place principles — a space that feels safe without you consciously knowing why.

Security integration

A panic button connects to trained, visible, non-police security partners. Prevention infrastructure — designed to hold space before anything happens.

Membership

Inclusivity from day one.

Emergency charging, the seating booth, and essential wellness items remain 100% free forever — for everyone, always, no membership required.
