The problem

Going out shouldn't feel like project management.

You shouldn't have to think about where your bag is, whether your phone will last, or how you're getting home. But right now, you do.

Hold your load

Secure, solar-powered pods hold your bag, jacket, shoes — whatever you brought but don't want to carry.

Top you up

Every pod has USB-C and Lightning charging built in. Your phone stays alive. Your night stays yours.

Get you home

One tap on the pod screen connects you to a verified ride. No searching, no surge surprises.

How it works

Three things. That's it.

1

Find a pod

VibeVault pods are positioned across Cape Town's promenades, waterfronts, and nightlife corridors. Look for the teal glow.

2

Drop, charge, sort

Tap the screen. Lock your bag. Plug in your phone. Done. The pod handles the rest while you handle the night.

3

Go home when you're ready

Collect your things, or book a ride directly from the pod screen. No apps to download, no accounts needed for basics.
