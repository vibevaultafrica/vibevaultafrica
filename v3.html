How it works

Simple by design.

01

Find a pod

VibeVault pods are placed at promenades, beaches, nightlife corridors, and popular destinations across Cape Town. Open the app or look for the teal glow.

02

Drop and go

Tap in, secure your bag or valuables, plug in your phone. The pod handles the rest while you enjoy the city — freely, fully, on your terms.

03

Head home safely

When you're ready, tap out, grab your gear, and the pod screen connects you straight to a verified ride home. Done.
